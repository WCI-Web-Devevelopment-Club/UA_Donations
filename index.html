ABOUT

What is happening?

On February 24th, Russia has invaded Ukraine. Many ukrainian civilians have died, fleed their homes and lost everything. People aroud the world are donating money and humanatarian aid toward ukraine, in order to support those affected by this devastating war.

Why Donate?

It is important to help people in such desperate times. By making a donation, you can contribute towards a great cause, by helping to save someone's life. This way, you can show your support for the Ukrainian people.

Who are we?

A group of Ukrainian families have partnered with HomeworkHub and Compass.Tutoring to raise donations in order to purchase products that will be shipped towards humanatarian aid to Ukraine. They will be flow to Poland and delivered to Ukraine.

Where Will My Donations Go?

  • Clothes for Men, Women and Children
  • Hygien Products for Men and Women
  • Blankets and Sleeping Bags
  • Disposable Tableware
  • Food

Our Process

  1. First, we receive as many donations as we can get.
  2. Then, at the end of each week, we will be buying supplies requested by the Ukranian Security Organization.
  3. All the products will be shiped to MEEST, which will sort them and fly them to Poland.
  4. From there, all the humanatarian aid will be delivered to Ukrainians in need.
